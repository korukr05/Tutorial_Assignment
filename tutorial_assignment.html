Video Embedding Tutorial

  1. Open Youtube or Vimeo website and find a video, you want to build into your webpage:
  2. Click on video,find share button and choose it
  3. Choose Embed
  4. In right side(in Youtube) copy generated code(iframe tag)
    In options you can contol time of video starting,
    if show elements of player contol and turn mode of extra privacy
  5. Paste copied code into webpage code
  6. Press keyboard combination Ctrl+S or choose "Save" option in File section
  7. Embed your own video
